Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your Room Booker account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or by contacting our support team.

Option 1: Delete via the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Room Booker app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon) on the main screen.
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete My Account.
  5. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm the permanent deletion.

Option 2: Manual Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request that we manually delete your account and data.

Please send an email to support@goforth.tech from the email address associated with your account with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

We will process your request within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been permanently removed from our systems.

What data will be deleted?

When you delete your account:

  • Your user profile and settings are removed.
  • All booking requests associated with your email address are deleted across all organizations.
  • Your authentication credentials are permanently removed from our secure identity provider (Firebase).

Note: If you own an organization, the organization itself and its shared history will remain, but your personal identifying information will be removed from it.
